Frequently asked

  • I bought "Remove ads" but still see ads. Open Settings inside the game and tap Restore purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
  • My purchase did not go through but I was charged. Purchases are handled by Apple. Tap Restore purchases first; if it still is not applied, email us with your receipt and we will sort it out.
  • I lost my progress. Progress is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes it. If something looks wrong, contact us.
  • How do I turn off ads / haptics / sound? Most options are in the in-game Settings screen. "Remove ads" is a one-time purchase.
  • How do I make and share my own levels? Tap Create level on the main menu, paint a puzzle, then publish it or share its code with friends.
  • Remove my shared content. Email us and we will delete any level or community data tied to your device.
