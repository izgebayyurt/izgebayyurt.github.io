Huemeld — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

The short version: Huemeld has no accounts, no sign-in, and no server of ours that collects your data. Your puzzle progress lives on your device. The free version shows ads through Google AdMob, which may use a device identifier for advertising if you allow tracking. Buying "Remove Ads" or "Everything" stops all ads.

What we store on your device

Huemeld saves your game locally (level progress, daily-puzzle streak, settings, and purchase unlock flags) using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device through us, and deleting the app deletes it.

Advertising (free version)

The free version of the iOS/Android app shows occasional interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect device information such as the advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, if you grant permission), IP address, and coarse ad-interaction data. On iOS we ask for tracking permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt; if you decline, ads still show but are not personalized using your identifier.

Google's practices are described in the Google Privacy Policy and AdMob data disclosures.

Purchases

In-app purchases ("Remove Ads", "Everything") are processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play — we never see your payment details. We use RevenueCat to validate purchases and restore them across reinstalls; RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-generated identifier and your purchase receipt, not your name or payment info. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

What we do not do

  • No accounts, emails, or passwords.
  • No analytics SDKs beyond what AdMob/RevenueCat need to function.
  • No selling of personal data.
  • No collection of contacts, photos, location, or microphone/camera data.

Children

Huemeld is a general-audience puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are configured through Google AdMob's standard general-audience settings.

The web version

The version playable in a browser at izgebayyurt.github.io shows no ads and contains no ad or purchase SDKs; it only uses local storage for progress, and is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub's privacy statement applies to hosting logs).

Your choices

  • Stop ads: buy "Remove Ads" or "Everything", or play the web version.
  • Withdraw tracking consent (iOS): Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Erase your data: delete the app (or clear site data in your browser).

Changes & contact

If this policy changes, the update will appear on this page with a new effective date. Questions: izgebayyurt@gmail.com.
