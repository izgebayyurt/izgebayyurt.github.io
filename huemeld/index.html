How to play

Only three paints exist — red, yellow, blue. Mix them into the colours you need, then group four to clear.

  • Move a square onto a different square to mix — the second tile becomes the blend (red + yellow = orange).
  • Connect four or more of one colour and they pop on their own. Chains score big.
  • Anything already mixed just swaps — slide circles around to line up your groups.
Tap a tile, then a neighbour — or drag onto it.
