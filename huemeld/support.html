Mix colors. Fill the board.

Need a hand? Email izgebayyurt@gmail.com and we'll get back to you, usually within a couple of days. Please include your device model and iOS version, and — if it's about a purchase — the email tied to your App Store account.

How to play

Draw a line out of each glowing circle. Where two lines meet in a junction, their colors meld into a new one — red + yellow makes orange, blue + yellow makes green, and so on. Light up every square with the right color and cover the whole board to win. Stuck? Tap Hint to reveal one pipe, or Undo / Reset to try again.

Purchases

I bought Remove Ads or Huemeld Pro but still see ads / locked levels.

Open Settings (the gear icon) and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you bought with. If it still doesn't apply, email us with your purchase date and we'll sort it out.

How do I restore purchases on a new phone?

Sign in with the same Apple ID, install Huemeld, then Settings → Restore Purchases. Non-consumable purchases (Remove Ads, Huemeld Pro) restore for free.

Can I get a refund?

Purchases are handled by Apple. Request refunds at reportaproblem.apple.com.

My progress

I deleted the app — is my progress gone?

If you're signed into iCloud, Huemeld backs up your progress to your private iCloud key-value store. Reinstall on the same Apple ID and your levels, streak, and unlocks come back on first launch. Progress is also kept locally, so it survives app updates.

Hints & ads

How do hints work?

Tap Hint to reveal a ghost of one pipe. On the free version you watch a short video to earn a hint; if you own Remove Ads or Huemeld Pro, hints are free with no video.

Why am I seeing ads?

The free version shows occasional ads after your first levels. Buy Remove Ads or Huemeld Pro to turn them off for good.

Settings

Settings (gear icon) has a volume slider, a sound toggle, a light/dark theme switch, and color-blind labels that mark each color with a letter.

Found a bug, or a level you think can't be solved?

Every level is verified solvable, but if one has you convinced otherwise, email us the level number (shown at the top of the board) and a screenshot — izgebayyurt@gmail.com.
