How to play

Drag a line out of each coloured square (a source). A square can send out up to 4 lines. A line reaches a same colour ✦ directly, or meets a different colour at a junction to mix and carry that blend to its ✦. Feed every circle, leave no loose ends, and fill every open square to win. Dark blocked tiles can't be crossed or filled.
