Mix the primaries, fill the board, and run each gate's colour through it.

How to play

Drag from a circle to draw a line.

When two lines meet, their colours mix.

Draw a line into each ✦ square with the matching colour to light it up. You might need to mix colours to get it.

A gate only lets its own colour pass.

Light up every ✦ square and cover the whole board to win! Leave no unconnected lines.
